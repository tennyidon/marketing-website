Secure Medical Records Transfer

We understand the need of practitioner and patients and strive to make a difference Learn more Team Citron

Issue

The current medical industry lacks a streamlined process for consultation and patient medical record management.

  • Medical paperwork management problem
  • Repetitive medical data collection from practitioner to practitioner
  • Lack of reliable individual practitioner review
  • Analogue medical form collection

Project Aim

The core goal of the system is to improve the patient experience whilst visiting practitioners.

  • Secure medical documentation transfer
  • Elimination of repetitive data collection
  • Convienent access to intervention information
  • Easy location and selection of practitioners

Development Methodology

Our team has selected Kanban and Pair programming as the Development Methodology for the HealthScout project
